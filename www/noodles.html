Time Taken;5 Mins

Serves:1 Person

Ingredients:

  • 1 Packet of Koka noodles
  • 100 ml of Boiling water
  • 1 Flavour packet

Steps Involved:

step 1 :place noodles in a bowl

step 2:boil kettles and add to noodles

step 3 :add a plate on top to seal in heat

step 4:wait 2 mins

step 5:drain out water !Carefully!

step 6:add a flavour packet

step 7:enjoy your noodles
